What is AeroPress?

Aeropress brews a rich, full bodied coffee and it's a great travel companion.

There are several reasons why AeroPress coffee tastes so good: Total immersion of the grounds in the water results in rapid yet robust extraction of flavor. Total immersion permits extraction at a moderate temperature, resulting in a smoother brew.
